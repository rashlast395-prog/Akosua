Questions

FAQ

What is your typical turnaround time?+

Turnaround depends on scope. A logo or social asset is typically 3–7 days. A full brand identity or explainer video is 2–4 weeks. I always communicate timelines clearly before starting.

Do you work with international clients?+

Yes. I work with clients globally and am comfortable with remote collaboration across time zones. Communication is clear, timely, and documented.

What file formats do you deliver?+

I deliver print-ready PDFs, transparent PNGs, high-res JPEGs, editable source files (AI, PSD, INDD), and optimized web formats. For video, I provide MP4, MOV, and social-ready cuts.
